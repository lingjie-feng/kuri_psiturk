Introduction
This study is part of a research study conducted by Dr. Henny Admoni at Carnegie Mellon University and is funded by NSF.
The purpose of the research is to evaluate and compare different social behaviors for robots.

Procedures
You will be asked to view images or videos of a robot and answer questions regarding them. This study may take up to 30 minutes.

Participant Requirements
Participation in this study is limited to individuals age 18 and older.

Risks
The risks and discomfort associated with participation in this study are no greater than those ordinarily encountered in daily life or during other online activities.

Benefits
There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study but the knowledge received may be of value to robotics research at Carnegie Mellon University.

Compensation & Costs
You will be compensated at a competitive Amazon Mechanical Turk rate. There will be no cost to you if you participate in this study.

Confidentiality
The data captured for the research does not include any personally identifiable information about you. Your IP address will not be captured. Study sponsors may have access to research records.
